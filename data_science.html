Become a Data Scientist

Our comprehensive data science program spans over four months, covering all the necessary foundations and advanced topics. We believe that this duration allows for a deep understanding and practical application of concepts, setting you up for success in the field of data science.
Through hands-on projects and real-world simulations, you will gain valuable experience that allows you to apply theoretical concepts to practical scenarios. This ensures that you have a strong foundation in data science and are well-prepared for real-world challenges

Despite being a remote program, we ensure that our classes have small sizes, allowing for personalized attention and direct interaction with our knowledgeable facilitators. This fosters a collaborative learning environment that enhances your understanding and progress.

We understand that our trainees have diverse schedules due to work or schooling commitments. That's why we offer a flexible training schedule that adapts to your availability. We believe that you should have the opportunity to pursue your passion for data science without compromising your other commitments.

Who is this Program For

New to Data Science

You are new to Data Science or come from a different industry but are looking to future-proof your skillset.

Flexible Schedule

You are looking for a programme that will work around your schedule and you can dedicate up to 35 hours per week to learning.

Inspired by Data

You are inspired by Big Data, Machine learning, or AI and have a strong desire to learn about how to use data to solve real-world problems.

How You’ll Learn

Work through downloadable content, Free Software and endless access to materials

Interact with your peers and mentors online or in-person.

Supplement your learning with interactive content including video lectures and walk-throughs.

Test your knowledge with weekly quizzes and projects based on the latest in tech.

Investigate real-world case studies and learn to apply your skills practically.

Fees & Financing

We offer flexible payment plans to make the program accessible and affordable for everyone. We believe that finances should not be a barrier to your educational journey, and we strive to provide options that suit your individual circumstances.

Programme Requirements

  • 18–34 years old
  • Ability to write and speak English proficiently
  • A laptop or desktop computer with access to a stable internet connection
