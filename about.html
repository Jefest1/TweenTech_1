Tween Tech

Providing in demand technology solutions

Tween Technologies is a technology company enthusiastic about advancing software and hardware development. We have achieved notable milestones in software development and have successfully trained individuals in this technological domain. We actively engage both individuals and groups in online training programs within the technology field. Tween Technologies, based in Ghana, specializes in consumer electronics, computer software, and online services.
